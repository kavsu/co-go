Research Project Milestones

Research Proposal

Our research topic is Adaptable Game. We Identified our research problem and we proposed the idea of developing a game that adjust the hardness level of the game by a prediction for improving attraction and business value as a solution. We decided to develop a game that uses the user’s characteristics as input, analyzes them and adapts according to the user’s mood. It was decided to use components such as Emotions from facial expression, Age, Gender, Face shape, Skill level from Haptic feedback, and Emotions from Heart rate as inputs. Four research areas were separately Identified and the overall process was explained and how they will be achieved.
